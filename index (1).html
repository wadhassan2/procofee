— Our Story

Born from a
passion for
the craft

Ember & Bean began as a dream in a tiny rented kitchen on a rainy Tuesday in London. Two friends. A second-hand roaster. And an obsession with finding the perfect cup.

Today, we source directly from twelve farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building lasting relationships with the families who tend each crop. We believe great coffee starts long before the grind.

Our shop is a sanctuary. Slow down. Stay a while.
